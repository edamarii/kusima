Pork Steak

Ulam Recipe

The first time my mother introduced me to this dish, I thought it was quite salty. As time went by, mama continued to cook this dish until she was able to cook it perfectly. She often cooked this for lunch. Everytime I remember this dish, I remember warm afternoons during summer break when I would be at home during lunch.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of pork chops
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 pieces calamansi
  • ½ cup cooking oil
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 ½ cups of water
  • ½ tsp granulated white sugar
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Put pork chop, soy sauce, and calamansi juice in a bowl. Cover bowl with a plastic wrap and let marinate for about an hour or more.
  2. Heat pan, then pour in cooking oil.
  3. In medium heat, fry the marinated pork chops in the pan. About 3 minutes per side.
  4. Remove excess oil from the pan, then pour the rest of the marinade. Add water and let boil. Let simmer until the pork is tender, and add water whenever needed.
  5. Add sugar, salt, and pepper then stir.
  6. Put the onions in the pan and let cook for 3 more minutes.
  7. Serve hot, and enjoy!
