How to operate a stove

If you have an induction cooker

Induction cookers use electricity to induce heat. There is no fire involved.

  • » Avoid induction cookers from making contact with water after cooking. Give your induction cooker time to cool down first.
  • » Induction cookers are energy-efficient, this means it reduces electricity consumption. Good for your electricity bills.
  • » Unplug induction cooker after use.

If you have a gaslit stove

  • » Always check on your LPG tank before, and after use.
  • » Always check if there are any holes in the tube. This is to ensure that there are no gas leaks.
  • » Always inspect when you smell gas.
  • » After use, make sure you 've turned off the knob that allows gas to go through the tube.
