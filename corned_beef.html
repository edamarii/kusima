Corned Beef

Agahan Recipe

You can have this as your ulam, however one of my dad’s favorite breakfast meals includes corned beef. So sometimes, if my mom can, then she cooks this for us. Sometimes, it would be leftovers from the night before. One thing I learned from my mom cooking corned beef over the years, was that you should choose your brand of corned beef according to preference. My mom prefers to mix two brands, instead of cooking just one.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 large potato, diced
  • 1 onion, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of corned beef
  • Half cup water
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Over medium heat, heat the oil on a skillet.
  2. Add-in potatoes until they are lightly brown.
  3. Add onions and garlic, stirring occasionally, until they are soft.
  4. Add the corned beef to the skillet. Afterwards, pour the water and let it boil. Break the meat into small pieces.
  5. Cover the skillet, and lower the heat. Let it simmer for about 3 to 5 minutes until the potatoes are soft
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot, and enjoy!
