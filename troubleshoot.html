Troubleshoot

The Basics

Have you ever put too much of an ingredient? Don't fret! For we have solutions that may help alleviate the results of pouring too much of n ingredient

Too much Soy Sauce

Introduce sweet or sour flavors to counteract the soy sauce's salty taste. You can use vinegar, lemon juice, or honey.


While cooking, you can also add more water to dilute.

Too much Salt

Much like soy sauce, you can use sweet or sour flavors to counteract the taste. You can use sugar, vinegar, or citrus juice like lemon or lime.

Too much Oil

You either scoop out the oil, or pour parts out.You can use paper towels to absorb food with too much oil on them.
