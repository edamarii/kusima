The Basic Filipino Breakfast

Agahan Recipe

Imagine waking up to the smell of freshly fried hotdogs, and eggs mixed with the aroma of garlic rice. It hits differently, especially, on extremely early mornings when you're about to go to school. Mama, when she had more time in the mornings, used to serve breakfast like this often. Itâ€™s memorable, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of hotdogs
  • 1 egg
  • (ingredients for sinangag)

Instructions

  1. Unwrap 2 pieces of hotdogs after defrosting them, and create 3 evenly spaced diagonal cuts on the hotdog.
  2. Pour cooking oil onto the pan,and turn the stove to medium heat. Wait for the pan to be warm enough before putting the hotdogs in.
  3. Pour cooking oil onto the pan,and turn the stove to medium heat. Wait for the pan to be warm enough before putting the hotdogs in.
  4. Put the stove on low heat. Crack an egg and slowly place into the pan to avoid breaking the yolk. As it cooks, scoop oil and sprinkle onto the egg yolk to cook the top of the egg better.
  5. Cook the egg until you get your desired result.
  6. Place on the plate, and add in sinangag (Sinangag recipe here).
  7. Serve hot, and enjoy!
