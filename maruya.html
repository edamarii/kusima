Merienda Recipe

Growing up, I always thought that this snack in the afternoon was banana pancakes. They reminded me of pancakes, but the outside it's crispy. But they were delicious, and hit the spot right on either rainy or sunny afternoons. Just chilling with my family in the living room, chatting.

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces of Saging na Saba
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¾ cup granulated white sugar
  • 1 cup fresh milk
  • ¾ cups cooking oil
  • ½ tsp of salt

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar into a big bowl and then mix.
  2. While mixing, mash the bananas as you go.
  3. Add-in the egg, vanilla extract, and then milk. Mix well.
  4. Heat the oil in a pan.
  5. When the oil becomes hot, add half a cup of the mixture in the pan. Pour oil into the pan as you go through the mixture.
  6. Fry both sides until the color turns brown. And sprinkle sugar on both sides.
  7. Serve hot, and enjoy!
