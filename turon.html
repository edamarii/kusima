Turon

Merienda Recipe

Oh the sweet, crunchy turon. A lovely snack that can even be eaten with powdered milk. My memories of turon date further back into my childhood, my mom used to do it more often back then. It was my childhood's favorite merienda that she made.

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces of Saging na Saba cut in lengthwise
  • 1 ½ cup sugar
  • 12 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 2 cups of cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Roll the banana on a plate of sugar, ensuring that the banana is coated with enough sugar.
  2. Put the sugar coated banana in a lumpia wrapper, and fold. Seal the wrapper with water.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan, and put in some sugar and wait until the brown sugar floats.
  4. Add in the wrapped banana and fry until golden brown. Add the extra sugar sticks on the wrapper.
  5. Serve hot, and enjoy!
