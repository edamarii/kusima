Beef with Broccoli

Ulam Recipe

I remember the reason why this became part of the roster of meals my mom prepares, was because I mentioned eating something like this in a restaurant. A few days later she served this meal to us for lunch. It made me happy to know my mom was listening, and now my siblings and I love this ulam!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef, slice into thin pieces
  • 2 cups broccoli cut into florets
  • 2 teaspoons of minced ginger
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • ½ to ¾ cups of water optional
  • ¼ cup cooking oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper
  • Marinade:
  • ¼ cup oyster sauce
  • 1tbsp knorr liquid seasoning
  • ½ tbsp sesame oil, opt.
  • 1 tsp granulated white sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix beef, and the marinade ingredients into a bowl. Let it marinate for 15 minutes before adding cornstarch. Mix well again, and set it aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a cooking pot, and saute ginger and garlic. Before the garlic turns brown, add in the broccoli, then stir fry for 1 to 2 minutes. Set aside.
  3. Pour the remaining oil into the pot and mix in the marinated beef once the oil is hot. Stir fry until the beef becomes brown. Then add water, let it boil and then evaporate. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the cooked broccoli into the pot with the beef. Cook and stir for another 3 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, and enjoy!
