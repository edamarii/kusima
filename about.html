About Us

As a kid growing up, my mother has always been the one who cooked the most in our house. It's one of the spaces in the house my entire family respects as hers. The systems established in the kitchen were managed by her, and the way things are organized were also by her. She corrects anyway, in case we fail to follow up on this system. Her food never fails to taste so delicious, and I know that one day when I leave the nest, is food that I will definitely miss going home to.

The Reason We Built Kusima

Kusima is to pay homage to her, my mother, Marites, and her love that we felt through her cooking. Aside from that, I would love to share these recipes for those who want to taste home cooked meals, and share a mother's affection.
